Mobile Number Sharing:`

Allow donors and receivers to share their contact numbers securely through the platform for direct communication.

Email Notifications:

Use email to notify donors about blood requests and receivers about donor availability.

Chat System:

Implement a secure in-app chat feature for real-time communication between donors and receivers.

Location Sharing:

Enable donors and receivers to share their locations to facilitate easy coordination for blood donation.

Request Forms:

Create a form where receivers can submit their blood requirements, which is then forwarded to matching donors.

Verification System:

Ensure both donors and receivers are verified through OTP (One-Time Password) on their mobile numbers or email.

Privacy Controls:

Allow users to control what contact information (e.g., phone number, email) they want to share.

Emergency Alerts:

Send SMS or push notifications to nearby donors in case of urgent blood requirements.

Feedback System:

Allow donors and receivers to leave feedback to improve trust and communication.

Support Hotline:

Provide a dedicated support number or email for resolving any issues between donors and receivers.
